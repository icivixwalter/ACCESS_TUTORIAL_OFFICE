Examples of printing orders

Informazioni specifiche sulla lingua

You can view and print your data in the direction, right-to-left or left-to-right, that you assigned to the object.

Objects that are longer or wider than can be printed on a single sheet of paper are printed on multiple sheets in an order that is determined by the view direction of the object. The following example shows the page numbering and printing order for a report that is three pages wide and requires six pages to print.

Object direction Multiple page printing order
Left-to-Right
Right-to-Left

Note   Reports and labels can be printed on a page in a multiple-column format. The layout of the columns will be determined by the direction of the report.
